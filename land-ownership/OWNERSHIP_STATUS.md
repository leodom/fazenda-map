## Legal Strategies to Consider

### For Rodrigo's Protection:
1. **Usucapião (Adverse Possession):**
   - If Rodrigo has worked the land continuously for required period
   - May establish ownership rights through use
   - Requires: continuous, peaceful, public possession with intent to own

2. **Formal Agreement:**
   - Negotiate written agreement with all legal owners
   - Establish exploitation rights, profit sharing, or lease terms
   - Protect investment in improvements

3. **Purchase Option:**
   - Right of first refusal if siblings want to sell
   - Negotiate purchase of shares over time

4. **Partnership/Company:**
   - Form legal entity for land exploitation
   - Distribute shares based on contribution (work vs ownership)

- Rural property law (direito agrário)
