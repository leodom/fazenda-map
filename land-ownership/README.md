## Legal Considerations

### Potential Legal Routes:

1. **Usucapião (Adverse Possession)**
   - Requires: continuous, peaceful, public possession
   - Duration: 5-15 years depending on type
   - Outcome: Ownership through use

2. **Family Agreement**
   - Negotiate exploitation rights
   - Profit-sharing arrangement
   - Right of first refusal if sale

3. **Purchase**
   - Buy out siblings' shares
   - Financing options
   - Valuation needed

4. **Partnership/Company**
   - Form legal entity
   - Distribute shares (work vs capital)
   - Formal profit distribution

⚠️ **All require legal consultation** - this is for information only
