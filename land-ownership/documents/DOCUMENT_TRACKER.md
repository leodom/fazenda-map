## How to Obtain Documents

### Cartório de Registro de Imóveis (Property Registry)
**Address:** Av. Lord Baden Powell, 45, Loja 02 - Centro, Cachoeiras de Macacu - RJ
**Phone:** (21) 2649-3139
**Documents:** Matrícula, Certidões, Averbações
**Cost:** Varies by document type
**Time:** Usually 3-10 days
**Required:** ID, property details (Matrícula nº 71)

### Cartório Civil (Civil Registry)
**Location:** Cachoeiras de Macacu (multiple locations)
**Documents:** Birth, death, marriage certificates
**Cost:** Modest fees
**Required:** Names, approximate dates, parents' names

### Receita Federal (Tax Authority)
**Online:** https://www.gov.br/receitafederal/
**Documents:** ITR declarations, CPF status
**Access:** Requires e-CPF or gov.br account
**Note:** Only owner can access their own records

### INCRA
**Regional Office:** [Need to identify for RJ]
**Documents:** CCIR, CAFIR, cadastral information
**Access:** May require property owner authorization

### Fórum (Court)
**Location:** Comarca de Cachoeiras de Macacu
**Documents:** Inventários, processes
**Access:** May require lawyer
**Note:** Old records may be archived
